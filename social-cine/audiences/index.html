FAQ

What to know before you come.

What if it rains?

Editions are rescheduled to the next available date. Your ticket carries over automatically — we'll email and WhatsApp you with the new date.

Age policy?

All ages welcome unless the film's rating restricts otherwise. 21+ ID required for the VIP welcome drink.

Can I bring outside food or drink?

BYOB tickets allow blankets but not outside food or drink. On-site vendors cover all dietary needs and pricing is competitive.

Accessibility?

Devon House has accessible parking and seating reserved on request. Email gabbie@screenshotinteractive.com to coordinate.

Parking at Devon House?

Free on-site parking. Arrive by 6pm for best parking.
