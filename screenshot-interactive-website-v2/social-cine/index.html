Running order

A premium night, on a schedule.

Doors at 5pm. Movie at 7pm. Social hour 'til close. Every Social Cine' runs the same rhythm — guests know exactly what to expect.

5:00 PM
Doors open

Vendors begin serving. Activations live. DJ on.

6:30 PM
Audience settles

Last call for food, drinks, activations.

6:45 PM
Pre-show content

Branded sponsor reels. Themed trivia. Behind-the-scenes.

7:00 PM
Feature presentation

The film begins.

~ Mid-feature
Interactive intermission

10–15 min break. Concessions, activations, audience giveaways.

9:30 PM
Social hour

Movie ends. DJ resumes. Activations stay open.

10:30 PM
Close

Final call. Guests depart.
