About

We started with one mirror.

Screenshot Interactive started with a single Magic Mirror booth and a hunch — that brands in Jamaica wanted activations, not just rentals. Today we run four lines of business: brand activations, a managed creator network, our live trivia property Screen Test, and our outdoor cinema series Social Cine'. Built for the brands you've heard of, the audiences they want to reach, and the moments people actually share.

What we believe

Three principles.

Participation beats interruption.

People scroll past ads. They don't scroll past competition. Everything we build is something people opt into — booths they line up for, creators they follow, quizzes they argue about, cinema nights they show up for.

Local creators tell local stories better.

Jamaican audiences respond to Jamaican voices. We don't import talent. We build it.

If it can't be measured, it doesn't ship.

Every activation captures data. Every event reports back. Every campaign closes with numbers. Sponsors don't buy hope.
